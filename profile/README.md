## 🚀 At a Glance
Gigapixel AI for Windows is Topaz Labs' image enhancement and upscaling application, designed to grow your photos in size while improving how they look. Ordinary enlargement stretches the pixels you already have and leaves images looking soft; Topaz Gigapixel AI takes a smarter route, using AI models to rebuild the fine detail a larger image demands. Whether you are enlarging a favorite shot for print, rescuing a small archived file, or upscaling a batch for a client, it delivers bigger pictures that stay clean and detailed.

## ❓ FAQ
**Is Gigapixel AI free?**
Gigapixel AI is a paid app from Topaz Labs, provided with a free trial so you can evaluate it first. The Topaz Gigapixel AI free trial lets you test enlargement and enhancement on your own photos; continued use requires a purchased license rather than a free full version.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
Gigapixel AI supports modern 64-bit Windows releases, including Windows 10 and Windows 11. Keeping Windows and your graphics drivers updated helps the AI upscaling run efficiently.

**Is my data secure?**
Your images are enhanced and upscaled locally on your Windows PC, so the photos stay on your machine during processing. For account, licensing, and privacy details, see the information supplied with the app.
